Zucchini Waffles

How to make healthier waffles with Zucchini!

These waffles will make you want to eat your veggies more.

These waffles you will not want to share...ever.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded zucchini
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Fourth teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • Half cup dry potato flakes
  • Fourth teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat waffles iron according to manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Mix zucchini, eggs, vegetable oil, onion powder, and salt together in a bowl. Stir in potato flakes and baking powder; mix until batter is combined.
  3. Pour half cup of the batter onto the center of the waffle iron. Close the lid; cook until iron stops steaming and waffle is crisp, about 5 minutes.
